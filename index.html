CodeCraft Academy

Who are we?

CodeCraft Academy is a non-profit organization created to provide the younger generation with computer science knowledge so they may later apply it in their lives. Through teaching at local public schools like Millikin Elementary School and hosting coding workshops in our local libraries, we have established a foundation for our nonprofit organization. With over 3 different tracks for classes, we have taught a total of 200 students in the Bay Area how to write their first program.

Our Service

We Are Committed To Giving Children the Best Start at Coding

At CodeCraft Academy, our mission is to educate the adolescents of our community about the fundamentals of computer science so they may later apply it later on. We make this accessible to anyone.

  • In-Person

    Taught 100 students aging from 8-13 years old in local elementary and middle schoolers.

  • Online

    Taught 50 students in our online classes aging from 6-13, taught Scratch and Python alternatively.

  • Events

    Hosted workshops in our local libraries, encouraging students to write their first program.

  • Hackathons

    Currently working on hosting hackathons in local high schools for students to participate in for great prizes!
